Team

We’re sustainable energy project developers

Mumuni Energy Corporation Limited is a Zambian registered, citizen owned business entity that has been setup to serve as a holding company for the development of sustainable power projects. Over the short to medium term, Mumuni’s main focus is the development of utility scale PV solar projects in Zambia and DRC.
