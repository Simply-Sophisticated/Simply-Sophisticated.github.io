About Us

We are a power developer in Southern Africa. Our key focus is PV solar power generation.

Mumuni Energy Corporation Limited is a Zambian registered, citizen owned business entity that has been setup to serve as a holding company for the development of sustainable power projects. Over the short to medium term, Mumuni’s main focus is the development of utility scale PV solar projects in Zambia and DRC.

Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable and cost-effective power to drive economic growth in Zambia and in the Southern African region. We aim to be the engine for growth in mining, manufacturing & agriculture sectors, and for social economic development in the region.

Vision

Our Vision is to be a leading player in the renewable & mainstream energy sector in the Zambia and in Southern Africa region. We aspire to be the Power Producer of choice for Bulk Power Off-takers in Zambia and in the region, providing reliable power at competitive rates.

Values

Our core values are excellence, integrity, innovation, diligence, accountability and partnership.

Contributing to economic development through sustainable energy solutions

Mumuni is participating in developing PV Solar plants in the country, in support of the nation’s energy generation development objectives. Our immediate objective is to complete development of the Chililabombwe 100MW PV Solar Power Project, to meet pressing demand on the mines in Zambia and DRC.

Developer of Sustainable Power Generation

Developing sustainable power generation in Zambia, DRC and Southern Africa region, in partnership with financing and EPC partners.

Our Goals

Below are our key short to medium term goals and objectives:

Chililabombwe PV Solar Plant

Complete development of the Chililabombwe 100MW PV Solar Power Project

Country-wide Solar Projects

Develop PV Solar Plants in at least two other regions in the country

Regional Expansion

Undertake joint venture projects for power generation in other countries within the Southern African region.

Nuclear Power Projects

Exploit SMR nuclear power to meet the country’s long term requirement for base load generation.

Coal Thermal Projects

Harness unrealised coal reserve. Seek partners with the start-of-the-art eco-friendly technologies to harness this resource.

Other Sustainable Projects

Explore other innovative renewable sources and seek partners to develop projects.
