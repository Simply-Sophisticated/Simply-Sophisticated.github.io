100MW Chililabombwe PV Solar Project

Chililabombwe 100MW PV Solar plant will supply mines in North-western & copperbelt provinces, and the DRC.

Locations:

Approximately 16km from Chililabombwe, Copperbelt province, Zambia. Site ooordinates at 12.43˚S, 27.95˚E. 150Ha has been set aside for the project.

Current Phase:

Pre-planning/Planning

Grid Connection:

CEC 220kV Grid within 2km of project site.

Funding

Mix of debt & equity financing from investment partners.

Target Market:
  • Mines in C/belt & north-western provinces, & DRC
  • Manufacturing industries
  • Southern African Power Pool (SAPP)
Offtakers:

Major utilities / large mines

Project Implementation

Our short term goal is successful implementation of Chililabombwe Solar PV Project across all its phases - from Planning, to Engineering, Construction, to Operation over the life of the plant.

Powering Industry...

Over and above the Chililabombwe Solar PV Project, Mumuni seeks to develop other similar utility scale projects in the country to address the huge power deficit currently obtaining in Zambia and in the Southern Africa region. The mines in the Copperbelt region of Zambia and DRC have a huge demand for power and therefore present a ready market, in addition to increasing household demand in both countries that remains unmet. In the medium term, Mumuni shall pursue opportunities in the wider Southern African region.
